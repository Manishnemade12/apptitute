## Quick Prep Strategy
1. 🔴 topics — practice **daily**, master shortcuts + solve 15-20 questions each.
2. 🟡 topics — solve 8-10 questions, know the standard approach.
3. 🟢 topics — just remember the formula, solve 2-3 questions to be safe.
