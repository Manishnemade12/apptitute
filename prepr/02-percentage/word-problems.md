# Word Problems

## Formulas
Population after n years = P * (1 + R/100)^n
Marks: Pass mark = (Marks scored + Marks failed by) OR (Marks scored - Marks above passing)

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Use 100 as initial value for easy calculation.

## Examples
**Q**: Candidate gets 20% marks and fails by 30 marks. Another gets 32% and passes by 42 marks. Find max marks.
**Ans**: Difference = 12% = 72 marks. Max marks = (72/12)*100 = 600.
