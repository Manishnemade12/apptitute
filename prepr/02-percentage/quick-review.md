# ⚡ Quick Review: Percentage 🔴

> All key formulas at a glance for last-minute revision.

---

## Basic Conversions
- x% = x/100 | Fraction → % = × 100 | % → Fraction = ÷ 100
- x% of y = y% of x (Symmetry property)

## Must-Know Fractions
| 1/2=50% | 1/3=33.33% | 1/4=25% | 1/5=20% | 1/6=16.66% |
|---------|-----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 1/7=14.28% | 1/8=12.5% | 1/9=11.11% | 1/10=10% | 1/11=9.09% |

---

## Percentage Change
```
% Change = (Difference / Original) × 100
```

## Reverse Percentage
- A is x% more than B → B is **x/(100+x) × 100 %** less than A
- A is x% less than B → B is **x/(100-x) × 100 %** more than A

| Pair | Reverse |
|------|---------|
| 10% more ↔ 9.09% less | 20% more ↔ 16.66% less |
| 25% more ↔ 20% less | 33.33% more ↔ 25% less |
| 50% more ↔ 33.33% less | 100% more ↔ 50% less |

---

## Successive Change
```
Net % = x + y + (xy/100)
```
- Same % up then down: Net = **-x²/100** (always decrease!)
- Price ↑ x%, to keep expenditure same: Reduce consumption by **x/(100+x) × 100 %**

## Word Problem Patterns
| Type | Key Formula |
|------|-------------|
| **Marks** | (b-a)% × Total = Failed by + Passed by |
| **Population** | P × (1 ± R/100)^n |
| **Depreciation** | V × (1 - R/100)^n |
| **Election (valid)** | Valid = Total × (100-Invalid%)/100, Diff = (2W-100)% × Valid |
| **Income** | Savings = Income - Expenditure, Savings% = (Savings/Income) × 100 |
| **Area** | L ↑ x%, B ↑ y% → Area change = x + y + xy/100 |
