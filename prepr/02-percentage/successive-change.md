# Successive Percentage Change

## Formulas
Net % change = x + y + (xy/100)

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 If both are increase, x and y are positive. If one is decrease, make it negative.

## Examples
**Q**: Salary increased by 10% then decreased by 10%.
**Ans**: 10 - 10 - (10*10/100) = -1% (1% decrease).
