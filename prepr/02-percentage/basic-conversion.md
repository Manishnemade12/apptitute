# Basic Percentage Conversion 🔴

## 📌 What is Percentage?
**Percentage** means "per hundred". It is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100.
```
x% = x/100
```

---

## 📝 All Conversion Formulas

### Fraction to Percentage
```
Percentage = Fraction × 100
```
Example: 3/5 = (3/5) × 100 = **60%**

### Percentage to Fraction
```
Fraction = Percentage / 100
```
Example: 45% = 45/100 = **9/20**

### Percentage to Decimal
```
Decimal = Percentage / 100
```
Example: 37.5% = 37.5/100 = **0.375**

### Decimal to Percentage
```
Percentage = Decimal × 100
```
Example: 0.064 = 0.064 × 100 = **6.4%**

### Finding x% of a Number
```
x% of N = (x/100) × N
```
Example: 20% of 350 = (20/100) × 350 = **70**

### What % is A of B?
```
Percentage = (A / B) × 100
```
Example: 45 is what % of 200? = (45/200) × 100 = **22.5%**

## 📝 Key Properties

| Property | Explanation |
|----------|-------------|
| x% of y = y% of x | 20% of 50 = 50% of 20 = 10 |
| 100% of anything = itself | 100% of 45 = 45 |
| x% of 100 = x | 37% of 100 = 37 |
| Percentages are ratios | 25% = 1:4 ratio |

## ⚡ Tricks & Shortcuts

> 💡 **Trick 1 – Reverse Calculation**: x% of y = y% of x. Use whichever is easier.
> - 17% of 200 = 200% of 17? No! But: 4% of 75 = 75% of 4 = **3** (much easier!)

> 💡 **Trick 2 – Break into Parts**: 
> - 15% = 10% + 5%
> - 37.5% = 25% + 12.5%
> - 17.5% = 10% + 5% + 2.5%

> 💡 **Trick 3 – Multiplier Approach**: Convert percentage to fraction for faster mental math.
> - 25% of 848 = 1/4 of 848 = **212**
> - 33.33% of 99 = 1/3 of 99 = **33**
> - 12.5% of 96 = 1/8 of 96 = **12**

> 💡 **Trick 4 – 1% Method**: Find 1% first, then scale.
> - 7% of 300 = 1% is 3, so 7% = 7 × 3 = **21**

## 📖 Solved Examples

### Example 1: Basic Conversion
**Q**: Express 5/8 as a percentage.  
**Solution**: (5/8) × 100 = **62.5%**

---

### Example 2: Finding Percentage
**Q**: What percentage of 240 is 36?  
**Solution**: (36/240) × 100 = **15%**

---

### Example 3: Using Fraction Shortcut
**Q**: Find 16.66% of 72.  
**Solution**: 16.66% = 1/6. So (1/6) × 72 = **12**

---

### Example 4: Reverse Property
**Q**: Find 8% of 25.  
**Solution**: 8% of 25 = 25% of 8 = (1/4) × 8 = **2**

---

### Example 5: Breaking Into Parts
**Q**: Find 35% of 460.  
**Solution**:  
- 10% of 460 = 46
- 30% = 46 × 3 = 138
- 5% = 46/2 = 23
- 35% = 138 + 23 = **161**

---

### Example 6: Word Problem
**Q**: In an exam, a student scores 432 out of 600. What is his percentage?  
**Solution**: (432/600) × 100 = **72%**

## 🎯 Exam Tips
- Memorize the fraction-percentage table — it saves 30+ seconds per question
- The "break into parts" method is the fastest for mental calculation
- Always simplify fractions before converting to percentage
