# Basic Percentage Conversion

## Formulas
Fraction to %: Multiply by 100
% to Fraction: Divide by 100

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Memorize fraction equivalents: 1/2=50%, 1/3=33.33%, 1/4=25%, 1/5=20%, 1/6=16.66%, 1/7=14.28%, 1/8=12.5%, 1/9=11.11%, 1/11=9.09%.

## Examples
**Q**: What is 16.66% of 36?
**Ans**: 1/6 * 36 = 6.
