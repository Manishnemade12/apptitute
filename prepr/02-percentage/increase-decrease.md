# Percentage Increase/Decrease

## Formulas
Percentage change = (Difference / Initial Value) * 100

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 If A is x% more than B, then B is (x/(100+x))*100 % less than A.

## Examples
**Q**: If price increases by 25%, by what % should consumption decrease to keep expenditure same?
**Ans**: (25/125)*100 = 20%.
