# Proportion

## Formulas
If a:b :: c:d, then a*d = b*c (Product of extremes = Product of means).
Third proportion of a, b is b^2/a.
Mean proportion of a, b is √(a*b).

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Direct variation: a1/b1 = a2/b2. Inverse variation: a1*b1 = a2*b2.

## Examples
**Q**: Find fourth proportional to 4, 9, 12.
**Ans**: 4*x = 9*12 -> x = 27.
