# Dividing a number in given ratio

## Formulas
If sum S is divided into a:b, then parts are S * (a/(a+b)) and S * (b/(a+b)).

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Find the value of 1 'unit' by dividing total sum by total ratio parts.

## Examples
**Q**: Divide 1000 in ratio 3:2.
**Ans**: Total parts = 5. 1 part = 200. Parts are 600 and 400.
