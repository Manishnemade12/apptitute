# ⚡ Quick Review: Probability 🟡

---

## Core: P(E) = Favorable / Total | P(E') = 1 - P(E)

## Sample Spaces
- 1 coin: 2 | n coins: 2ⁿ | 1 die: 6 | 2 dice: 36 | Cards: 52

## Cards Deck
4 suits × 13 each | Red=26, Black=26 | Face cards=12 | Aces=4

## Rules
- AND: P(A)×P(B) [independent] | OR: P(A)+P(B)-P(A∩B)
- At least one = **1 - P(none)** | Conditional: P(A∩B)/P(B)
