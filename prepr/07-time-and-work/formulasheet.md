# Time & Work – Complete Formula Sheet (Aptitude)

> Covers almost all Time & Work questions asked in TCS, Infosys, Accenture, Capgemini, Cognizant, Wipro, SSC, Banking, Railways, and other aptitude exams.

---

# Basic Terms ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Total Work

```text
The entire work is assumed as 1 unit.

(LCM method is generally preferred in aptitude.)
```

### Work Done

```text
Work = Efficiency × Time
```

### Efficiency

```text
Efficiency = Work / Time
```

---

# 1. Work Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Work = Efficiency × Time
```

### Example

```text
Efficiency = 4 units/day

Time = 5 days

Work

= 4 × 5

= 20 units
```

---

# 2. Time Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time = Work / Efficiency
```

### Example

```text
Work = 60 units

Efficiency = 12 units/day

Time

= 60 / 12

= 5 days
```

---

# 3. Efficiency Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Efficiency = Work / Time
```

### Example

```text
Work = 100 units

Time = 20 days

Efficiency

= 100 / 20

= 5 units/day
```

---

# 4. One Day's Work ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

If a person completes work in **n days**

```text
One Day's Work = 1 / n
```

### Example

```text
A completes a work in 12 days.

One Day's Work

= 1 / 12
```

---

# 5. Combined Work ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

If

```text
A = 1/x

B = 1/y
```

Then

```text
(A + B)

= (x + y)

÷ (xy)
```

Combined Time

```text
= xy / (x + y)
```

### Example

```text
A = 10 days

B = 15 days

Combined Time

= (10 × 15)

÷ (10 + 15)

= 150 / 25

= 6 days
```

---

# 6. Three Persons Working Together ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Total Work Per Day

= 1/x + 1/y + 1/z
```

Time Required

```text
= 1

÷ (1/x + 1/y + 1/z)
```

### Example

```text
A = 12 days

B = 18 days

C = 36 days

One Day Work

= 1/12 + 1/18 + 1/36

= 6/36

= 1/6

Time

= 6 days
```

---

# 7. Efficiency Ratio ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Efficiency

∝ 1 / Time
```

or

```text
Efficiency Ratio

= Time Ratio (Reversed)
```

### Example

```text
A : B time

= 6 : 9

Efficiency

= 9 : 6

= 3 : 2
```

---

# 8. Time Ratio ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time

∝ 1 / Efficiency
```

### Example

```text
Efficiency

= 4 : 5

Time

= 5 : 4
```

---

# 9. Men and Work ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Men × Days = Constant
```

### Example

```text
20 men complete work in 15 days.

30 men will complete it in

(20 × 15) / 30

= 10 days
```

---

# 10. Men, Days & Hours ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Men × Days × Hours

= Constant
```

### Example

```text
10 men

8 hours/day

12 days

Same work by

20 men

6 hours/day

Required Days

= (10×8×12)

÷ (20×6)

= 8 days
```

# 11. Pipes & Cistern ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Filling Pipe

```text
One Day Work

= 1 / Filling Time
```

### Emptying Pipe

```text
One Day Work

= −1 / Emptying Time
```

### Combined Work

```text
Add Filling Pipes

Subtract Emptying Pipes
```

### Example

```text
Pipe A fills in 6 hours

Pipe B empties in 12 hours

Net Work

= 1/6 − 1/12

= 1/12

Tank fills in

12 hours
```

---

# 12. LCM Method ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Take Total Work

= LCM of all given times
```

### Example

```text
A = 12 days

B = 18 days

LCM

= 36 units

A's Efficiency

= 36/12

= 3 units/day

B's Efficiency

= 36/18

= 2 units/day

Together

= 5 units/day

Time

= 36/5

= 7.2 days
```

# 13. Wages Distribution ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Wages

∝ Work Done
```

If time is same,

```text
Wages

∝ Efficiency
```

### Example

```text
Efficiency

= 2 : 3

Total Wages

= ₹2500

Distribution

= ₹1000 : ₹1500
```

# Quick Tips

- ✅ Use the **LCM Method** whenever multiple people have different completion times—it is the fastest approach for aptitude.
- ✅ **Efficiency and Time are inversely proportional.**
- ✅ If one person takes **x days**, his **one day's work is 1/x**.
- ✅ For **Pipes & Cistern**, filling pipes are **positive (+)** and emptying pipes are **negative (−)**.
- ✅ In **Men & Work** questions, use **Men × Days = Constant**.
- ✅ In **Men, Days & Hours** questions, use **Men × Days × Hours = Constant**.
- ✅ **Wages are always divided in the ratio of work done (or efficiency if time is same).**
