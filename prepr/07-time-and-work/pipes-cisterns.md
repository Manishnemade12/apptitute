# Pipes and Cisterns

## Formulas
Same as Time & Work. Outlet pipes have negative efficiency.

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Take negative efficiency for leaks.

## Examples
**Q**: Pipe A fills in 10hr. Leak empties in 15hr. Total time?
**Ans**: Work=30. A eff=+3, Leak=-2. Net eff=1. Time=30/1 = 30hrs.
