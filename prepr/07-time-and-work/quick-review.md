# Quick Review: Time and Work 🔴

### Basic Work-Efficiency
**Formulas**:
Work = Time * Efficiency
**Trick**: Always assume Total Work = LCM of given days.

---
### Combined Work
**Formulas**:
1/A + 1/B = 1/T
**Trick**: For two people: (A*B)/(A+B) days.

---
### Pipes and Cisterns
**Formulas**:
Same as Time & Work. Outlet pipes have negative efficiency.
**Trick**: Take negative efficiency for leaks.

---
### Work Equivalence
**Formulas**:
(M1 * D1 * H1) / W1 = (M2 * D2 * H2) / W2
**Trick**: MDH / W rule.
