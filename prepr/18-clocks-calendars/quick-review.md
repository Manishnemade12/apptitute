# ⚡ Quick Review: Clocks & Calendars 🟢

---

## Clock
```
Angle = |30H - 5.5M|    (if >180, use 360-result)
```
- Hands overlap: M = 60H/11 (11 times in 12 hrs)
- Right angle: 22 times in 12 hrs | Opposite: 11 times

## Calendar
- Normal year = 1 odd day | Leap year = 2 odd days
- Century: 100yr=5, 200yr=3, 300yr=1, 400yr=0 odd days
- 0=Sun, 1=Mon ... 6=Sat
- Leap: div by 4 (yes) → div by 100 (no) → div by 400 (yes)
