# Change in Average

## Formulas
New Value = Old Average + Number of obs * Change in average

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 If a person is replaced and avg increases by x, New person = Old person + (n * x).

## Examples
**Q**: Avg age of 10 students is 15. Teacher included, avg becomes 16.
**Ans**: Teacher's age = 16 + (10 * 1) = 26.
