# Simple Average

## Formulas
Average = Sum of observations / Number of observations

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 If every observation is increased by x, the new average also increases by x.

## Examples
**Q**: Average of first 5 natural numbers?
**Ans**: (1+2+3+4+5)/5 = 3. Trick: For AP, Average = (First + Last)/2 = (1+5)/2 = 3.
