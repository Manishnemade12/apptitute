# Quick Review: Time, Speed & Distance 🔴

### Basic Speed Distance Time
**Formulas**:
Distance = Speed * Time
**Trick**: kmph to m/s: Multiply by 5/18.
m/s to kmph: Multiply by 18/5.

---
### Relative Speed
**Formulas**:
Opposite direction: Add speeds (S1 + S2)
Same direction: Subtract speeds (S1 - S2)
**Trick**: Used when two bodies are in motion.

---
### Train-based Problems
**Formulas**:
Time = (Length of Train + Length of Platform) / Speed
For standing man/pole, Length of Platform = 0.
**Trick**: Convert all units to m/s and meters before calculating.

---
### Boats and Streams
**Formulas**:
Downstream Speed (D) = Boat(u) + Stream(v)
Upstream Speed (U) = Boat(u) - Stream(v)
u = (D+U)/2, v = (D-U)/2
**Trick**: D is always greater than U.
