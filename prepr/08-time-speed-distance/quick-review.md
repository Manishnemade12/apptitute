# ⚡ Quick Review: Time, Speed & Distance 🔴

---

## Core: D = S × T
- km/hr → m/s: **× 5/18** | m/s → km/hr: **× 18/5**
- Speed ratio a:b (same dist) → Time ratio b:a

## Quick Conversions
| 18→5 | 36→10 | 54→15 | 72→20 | 90→25 | 108→30 |

---

## Relative Speed
- **Opposite direction**: S₁ + S₂
- **Same direction**: |S₁ - S₂|
- Meeting time = Distance / Relative speed

## Trains
| Scenario | Distance | Speed |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Pole/Person | L_train | S_train |
| Platform | L_train + L_platform | S_train |
| Another train (opposite) | L₁ + L₂ | S₁ + S₂ |
| Another train (same) | L₁ + L₂ | S₁ - S₂ |

**Finding L & S**: Crosses pole in t₁, platform(L) in t₂ → S = L/(t₂-t₁), L_train = S×t₁

---

## Boats & Streams
```
Downstream = u + v    |    Upstream = u - v
u = (D+U)/2    |    v = (D-U)/2
```
Round trip time = d/(u+v) + d/(u-v)
