# Boats and Streams

## Formulas
Downstream Speed (D) = Boat(u) + Stream(v)
Upstream Speed (U) = Boat(u) - Stream(v)
u = (D+U)/2, v = (D-U)/2

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 D is always greater than U.

## Examples
**Q**: Boat speed 10, Stream speed 2. Downstream time for 36km?
**Ans**: D = 12 kmph. Time = 36/12 = 3 hrs.
