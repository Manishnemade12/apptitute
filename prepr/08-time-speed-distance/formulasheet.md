# Time, Speed & Distance – Complete Formula Sheet (Aptitude)

> Covers almost all Time, Speed & Distance questions asked in TCS, Infosys, Accenture, Capgemini, Cognizant, Wipro, SSC, Banking, Railways, and other aptitude exams.

---

# Basic Terms ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Distance (D)

```text
Total path travelled by an object.
```

### Speed (S)

```text
Distance travelled per unit time.
```

### Time (T)

```text
Time taken to cover a given distance.
```

---

# 1. Basic Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

0

### Formula

```text
Distance = Speed × Time
```

or

```text
D = S × T
```

### Example

```text
Speed = 60 km/h

Time = 4 h

Distance

= 60 × 4

= 240 km
```

---

# 2. Speed Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Speed = Distance / Time
```

### Example

```text
Distance = 300 km

Time = 5 h

Speed

= 300 / 5

= 60 km/h
```

---

# 3. Time Formula ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time = Distance / Speed
```

### Example

```text
Distance = 240 km

Speed = 80 km/h

Time

= 240 / 80

= 3 h
```

---

# 4. Unit Conversion ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
1 km/h = 5/18 m/s
```

```text
1 m/s = 18/5 km/h
```

### Example

```text
72 km/h

= 72 × 5 / 18

= 20 m/s
```

```text
25 m/s

= 25 × 18 / 5

= 90 km/h
```

---

# 5. Relative Speed ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Same Direction

```text
Relative Speed

= Difference of Speeds
```

```text
= S₁ − S₂
```

### Opposite Direction

```text
Relative Speed

= Sum of Speeds
```

```text
= S₁ + S₂
```

### Example

```text
60 km/h and 40 km/h

Same Direction

= 20 km/h

Opposite Direction

= 100 km/h
```

---

# 6. Average Speed ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Equal Distances

```text
Average Speed

= (2 × S₁ × S₂)

÷ (S₁ + S₂)
```

### Example

```text
60 km/h and 40 km/h

Average Speed

= (2×60×40)

÷ (60+40)

= 48 km/h
```

---

# 7. Average Speed (General Case) ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Average Speed

= Total Distance

÷ Total Time
```

### Example

```text
120 km in 2 h

180 km in 3 h

Average Speed

= (120+180)

÷ (2+3)

= 60 km/h
```

---

# 8. Train Crossing a Pole ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time

= Length of Train

÷ Speed
```

### Example

```text
Train Length = 180 m

Speed = 20 m/s

Time

= 180 / 20

= 9 s
```

---

# 9. Train Crossing a Platform ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time

= (Train Length + Platform Length)

÷ Speed
```

### Example

```text
Train = 180 m

Platform = 120 m

Speed = 20 m/s

Time

= (180+120)

÷ 20

= 15 s
```

---

# 10. Two Trains Crossing ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Same Direction

```text
Time

= Sum of Lengths

÷ Difference of Speeds
```

### Opposite Direction

```text
Time

= Sum of Lengths

÷ Sum of Speeds
```

---

# 11. Boat & Stream ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Downstream Speed

```text
Boat Speed + Stream Speed
```

### Upstream Speed

```text
Boat Speed − Stream Speed
```

---

# 12. Boat Speed in Still Water ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Boat Speed

= (Downstream + Upstream)

÷ 2
```

---

# 13. Stream Speed ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Stream Speed

= (Downstream − Upstream)

÷ 2
```

### Example

```text
Downstream = 18 km/h

Upstream = 10 km/h

Boat Speed

= (18+10)/2

= 14 km/h

Stream Speed

= (18−10)/2

= 4 km/h
```

---

### Formula

```text

∝ 1 / Time
```

### Example

```text
Speed Ratio

= 3 : 4

Time Ratio

= 4 : 3
```

---

# 15. Time Ratio ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Formula

```text
Time

∝ 1 / Speed
```

---

# 16. Percentage Change in Speed & Time ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### If Speed increases by x%

```text
Time decreases by

(100x)

÷ (100 + x) %
```

### If Speed decreases by x%

```text
Time increases by

(100x)

÷ (100 − x) %
```

### Example

```text
Speed increases by 25%

Time decreases by

(100×25)

÷ 125

= 20%
```

# Quick Tips

- ✅ Always convert **km/h ↔ m/s** before solving train problems.
- ✅ **Relative Speed** uses **difference** for the same direction and **sum** for opposite directions.
- ✅ In train questions, use the **total distance crossed** (train length, platform length, or sum of train lengths).
- ✅ Use the **Average Speed** formula only when **equal distances** are covered.
- ✅ In Boat & Stream questions:
  - **Downstream = Boat + Stream**
  - **Upstream = Boat − Stream**
- ✅ **Speed and Time are inversely proportional** for the same distance.
