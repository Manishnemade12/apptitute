# Train-based Problems

## Formulas
Time = (Length of Train + Length of Platform) / Speed
For standing man/pole, Length of Platform = 0.

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Convert all units to m/s and meters before calculating.

## Examples
**Q**: Train 200m long at 72kmph crosses a 100m bridge. Time?
**Ans**: Speed=20m/s. Distance=300m. Time = 300/20 = 15s.
