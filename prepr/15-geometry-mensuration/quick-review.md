# ⚡ Quick Review: Geometry & Mensuration 🟡

---

## 2D Shapes
| Shape | Area | Perimeter |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Rectangle | l×b | 2(l+b) |
| Square | a² | 4a |
| Triangle | ½bh | a+b+c |
| Equilateral △ | (√3/4)a² | 3a |
| Circle | πr² | 2πr |
| Trapezium | ½(a+b)×h | sum of sides |
| Rhombus | ½d₁d₂ | 4a |

## 3D Shapes
| Shape | Volume | TSA |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Cube | a³ | 6a² |
| Cuboid | lbh | 2(lb+bh+lh) |
| Cylinder | πr²h | 2πr(r+h) |
| Cone | ⅓πr²h | πr(l+r) |
| Sphere | ⁴⁄₃πr³ | 4πr² |
| Hemisphere | ⅔πr³ | 3πr² |

## Key: Cone:Cylinder:Sphere volume = **1:3:2** (same r, h=2r)
