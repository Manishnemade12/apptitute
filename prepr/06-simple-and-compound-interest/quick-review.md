# Quick Review: Simple and Compound Interest 🔴

### SI Formula-based
**Formulas**:
SI = (P * R * T) / 100
Amount = P + SI = P * (1 + RT/100)
**Trick**: SI is same for every year.

---
### CI Formula-based
**Formulas**:
Amount = P * (1 + R/100)^T
CI = Amount - P
**Trick**: Use effective rate % (Successive percentage). For 2 years at 10%, effective CI% = 10+10+(10*10)/100 = 21%.

---
### SI vs CI Difference
**Formulas**:
2 years diff = P * (R/100)^2
3 years diff = P * (R/100)^2 * (3 + R/100)
**Trick**: Memorize these formulas, they appear in 90% tests.
