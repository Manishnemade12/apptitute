# ⚡ Quick Review: Simple & Compound Interest 🔴

---

## Simple Interest
```
SI = PRT/100    |    Amount = P(1 + RT/100)
P = 100×SI/(RT)    |    R = 100×SI/(PT)    |    T = 100×SI/(PR)
```
- SI is constant every year = PR/100
- Doubles in 100/R years | Triples in 200/R years

---

## Compound Interest
```
A = P(1 + R/100)^T    |    CI = A - P
```
| Compounding | Replace |
|-------------|---------|
| Half-yearly | R→R/2, T→2T |
| Quarterly | R→R/4, T→4T |

### Effective 2-Year CI%
```
Effective = 2R + R²/100
```
At 10% → 21% | At 20% → 44% | At 5% → 10.25%

### Rule of 72 (Doubling time ≈ 72/R)

---

## CI - SI Difference ⭐
```
2 years: CI - SI = P × (R/100)²
3 years: CI - SI = P × (R/100)² × (3 + R/100)
```
Given diff → P = Diff/(R/100)² | R = √(Diff/P) × 100

---

## Key Relationships
| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| CI Year 1 = SI Year 1 | No difference for 1st year |
| CI > SI for T ≥ 2 | Always |
| CI of nth year / (n-1)th year | = (1 + R/100) |
| Different rates | A = P(1+R₁/100)(1+R₂/100) |
