# LCM & HCF

## Formulas
- Product of two numbers = LCM * HCF
- LCM of fractions = LCM(Numerators) / HCF(Denominators)
- HCF of fractions = HCF(Numerators) / LCM(Denominators)

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 - To find largest number that divides A, B, C leaving remainders r1, r2, r3: Find HCF of (A-r1, B-r2, C-r3).
- To find smallest number divisible by A, B, C leaving remainder r in each case: Find LCM(A,B,C) + r.

## Examples
**Q**: Find LCM of 12 and 15.
**Ans**: 60.

**Q**: Two numbers are in ratio 3:4 and their HCF is 4. Find LCM.
**Ans**: Numbers are 12 and 16. LCM = 48.
