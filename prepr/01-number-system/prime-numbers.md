# Prime Numbers, Factors, Multiples

## Formulas
- Prime number has exactly two factors: 1 and itself.
- Co-primes have HCF = 1.

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 To check if N is prime, find integer k > √N. Check divisibility of N by all prime numbers <= k.

## Examples
**Q**: Is 137 prime?
**Ans**: √137 < 12. Primes <= 11 are 2, 3, 5, 7, 11. 137 is not divisible by any of these. So, yes.
