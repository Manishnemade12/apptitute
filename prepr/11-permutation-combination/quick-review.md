# ⚡ Quick Review: Permutation & Combination 🟡

---

## Formulas
```
nPr = n!/(n-r)!    |    nCr = n!/[r!(n-r)!]
```
- nC0 = nCn = 1 | nCr = nC(n-r) | nPn = n!

## Key Concepts
| Keyword | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Arrange/Order | Permutation |
| Select/Choose | Combination |
| At least one | Total - None |
| Together | Treat as block |
| Not together | Total - Together |

## Word Arrangements
- All different: **n!** | Repeated letters: **n!/(p₁!×p₂!...)**
- Vowels together: **(units)! × (vowels within)!**
- Circular: **(n-1)!** | Necklace: **(n-1)!/2**
