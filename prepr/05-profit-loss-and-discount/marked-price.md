# Marked Price Problems

## Formulas
SP = MP - Discount
Discount% = (Discount / MP) * 100
MP/CP = (100 + Profit%) / (100 - Discount%)

## Tricks & Shortcuts
> 💡 Use the MP/CP ratio formula for lightning fast answers.

## Examples
**Q**: Shopkeeper gives 10% discount and earns 20% profit. If CP is 300, find MP.
**Ans**: MP/300 = 120/90 = 4/3. MP = 400.
